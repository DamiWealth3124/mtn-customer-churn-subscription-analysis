**Business Problem / Objective**

MTN aims to reduce customer churn while improving subscription adoption and revenue performance across different regions, devices, and subscription plans.

Telecommunication businesses face constant customer switching due to pricing, service quality, and network experience. Understanding why customers churn, who is most at risk, and what drives retention is critical to sustaining long-term revenue growth.

This project answers the key business question:

How can MTN reduce customer churn and improve subscription performance using customer, device, and geographic insights?
